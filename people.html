Lab Members

Genevieve Stein-O'Brien, Ph.D. -- Kavli NDI Postdoctoral Fellow

Dr. Stein-O'Brien is a an Assistant Professor of Neuroscience Department at Johns Hopkins University with affiliations in the Data Science Division of the Department of Oncology, the McKusick-Nathans Institute of Genomics Medicine, the Institute for Data Intensive Engineering and Science, and the Kavli NDS Institute. She received a PhD in Human Genetics from Johns Hopkins School of Medicine and a MHS in Biostatistics from Johns Hopkins School of Public Health. Dr. Stein-O'Brien's research is focused on the stochastic processes underlying gene regulation in dynamic biological systems. Specifically, she develops computational and statistical methods for pattern recognition, latent space detection, and data integration in high-throughput omics.
