Lab Members

Genevieve Stein-O'Brien, Ph.D. -- Kavli NDI Postdoctoral Fellow

Dr. Stein-O'Brien is an Assistant Professor in the Neuroscience Departments at Johns Hopkins University with affiliations in the Division of Quantitative Sciences in the Department of Oncology, the McKusick-Nathans Department of Genomics Medicine, the Institute for Data Intensive Engineering and Science and the Kavli Neuroscience Discovery Institute. Dr. Stein-O'Brien's research is focused on the stochastic processes underlying gene regulation in dynamic biological systems. Specifically, she develops computational and statistical methods for pattern recognition, latent space detection, and data integration in high-throughput omics.

Asher Baraban -- Intern, Software Development

Asher is currently an undergraduate student at WashU in St. Louis, MO. He has been developing scProject, a single cell transfer learning tool for visualization and exploration of latent space utilization.
