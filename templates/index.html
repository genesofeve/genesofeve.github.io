The Goff Lab

McKusick-Nathans Institute of Genetic Medicine

The Goff Lab at Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine studies the regulatory role of non-coding RNAs in the establishment of cell identity during differentiation. We explore these questions in the context of the mammalian brain; an organ system with an incredible diversity of cell types.
