News/Honors

7/12/2016: Investigator-initiated Award from Johns Hopkins Science of Learning Institute

In collaboration with the lab of Dr. Solange Brown, the Goff Lab was recently awarded a 2016 JHU Science of Learning award to study Cell-type specific heterogeneity in experience-induced gene expression. This award provides support for the two labs to dissect the cell-type- and sensory-modality-specific responses to experience dependent plasticity in selected subsets of corticothalamic projection neurons.

6/20/2016: Investigator-initiated Award from MSRCF

The Goff Lab was recently awarded a 2016 MSCRF Investigator-initiated award for Single cell analysis of hippocampal neurogenesis defects in Kabuki Syndrome 1. This award will fund single-cell iPSC research into the genetic effects of mutations associated with the learning and memory deficits in Kabuki Syndrome.

Loyal Goff, 2017
